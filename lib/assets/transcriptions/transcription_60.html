A ratio is pencilled in lower left margin. In the text a pencilled colon separates the consecutive words "the" and "pores." As with the other markings of this sort, the 1853 edition separates these two words in the reset pagination. The words are located in that edition on pages 32 and 33 respectively; at the bottom of page 33, the signature "3" appears.
