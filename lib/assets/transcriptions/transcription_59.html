  • + five deluges are enumerated by Xenophon, the author of the tract de Aequivocis in these words. 'Inundationes plures fuere. prima novimestris inundatio terrarum, sub prisco Ogyge. secunda Niliaca menstrua, sub Aegyptiis Hercule et Prometheo. bimestris autem, sub Ogyge Attico in Achaia. trimetris Thessalica, sub Deucalione.
