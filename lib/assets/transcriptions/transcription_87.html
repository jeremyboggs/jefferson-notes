All three markings in pencil. The two pluses connect a printed margin note and an in-text reference to Buffon. The ratio gives a page number and a signature (see our explanation at page 26). There appear two dots in the text between "moisture" and "that" which represents the typesetting and pagination in the 1853 edition: "moisture" is the last word, in the main text, to appear on page 48 and "that" is the first to appear on page 49 of the new edition. The signature, "4", appears below the text and footnotes on page 49 of the 1853 edition.
