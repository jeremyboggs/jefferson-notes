The pluses are in pencil. Jefferson's manuscript addition, in ink, appears in the upper right-hand margin. On page 77 of Vespucci's Vita e lettere "lioni" and "porci salvatici" are listed and the snake's measurements given. On page 111, Vespucci provides another list of animals including both "leoni" and "orsi."
