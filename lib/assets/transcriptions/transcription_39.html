    2. Epoq. 317.
The margin note, in ink, is located in the center of the right margin. Jefferson may have originally tried to interlineate his manuscript text, could not fit it, and scratched out the annotation: some of the neighboring printed text has been erased and re-inked. "Epoq." is an abbreviation of Buffon's Époques de la nature (Epochs of Nature), a widely-read multivolume natural history. As Buffon was an important interlocutor for Jefferson, citations of his work appear throughout the Notes; in fact, a copy of the Notes was presented to Buffon by Jefferson in 1787. At the bottom of the page the residue of a red seal (originally attached to the opposite tipped-in leaf) has stuck to the surface of the paper.
