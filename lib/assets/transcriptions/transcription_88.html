A pencilled plus connects the note, in ink, to a quotation in the text that ends "les plus grands de tous." Maximilian Schele de Vere translates Buffon's French as follows: "'All that is colossal and grand in Nature has been formed at the North.' — 1. Epoq., 255. 'It is in our Northern regions that living nature has risen to the largest dimensions.'—lb. 263."
