At the end of the short paragraph beginning "Our changes from heat to cold," Jefferson inserts a colon, in ink, and lengthens the sentence. After following the printed line, this addition spills into the left margin. We wonder if Jefferson is struck by the numerology of 1774, 1784, and July 4, making the radical swing in temperature portentous of something more than meteorology.
