• Note. this description was written after a lapse of several years from the time of my visit to the bridge, and under an error of recollection which requires apology. for it is from the bridge itself that the mountains are visible both ways, and not from the bottom of the fissure, as my impression then was. the statement therefore in the former edition needs the corrections here given to it. Aug. 16. 1817.
