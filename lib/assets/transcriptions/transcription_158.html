A plus sign, in pencil, followed by the instruction "foot note" appears above Jefferson's manuscript additions, in ink, which supply missing census data. Another plus sign, in pencil, appears below the first column of the printed table on census results. In the 1853 edition, this supplementary data appears beneath the table.
